AlgoKittens are Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) on Algorand.

Each AlgoKitten is unique and has it's own MEOW token.

Each token links to an unique IPFS CID.

The first 10 AlgoKittens were minted on the 19th of April 2021.

How can I get some MEOW tokens?

All AlgoKittens are distributed through Reddit and Twitter giveaways.
